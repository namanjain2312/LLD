Functional Requirements
    a>N x N board (usually 100 cells)
    b>Players take turns rolling a dice (1-6)
    c>Snakes send you down the board (head → tail)
    d>Ladders send you up the board (bottom → top)
    e>First player to reach the final cell wins
    f>If a player rolls a 6, they get an extra turn

Objects/Classes
    a>Game
    b>Board
    c>Snake
    d>Ladder
    e>Player
    f>Dice
